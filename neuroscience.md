# 神经科学 / Neuroscience

### amygdala
- 分类：神经科学
- 词性：noun / 名词
- English explanation: The amygdala is an almond-shaped gray-matter structure in each temporal lobe/limbic system, linked to emotion—especially fear and pleasure—memory, and olfaction.
- 中文解释：杏仁核：位于颞叶前部、形似杏仁的灰质结构，属于边缘系统；与情绪（尤其恐惧、愉悦）、记忆和嗅觉相关。
- 词典/公开来源：
  - Merriam-Webster: an almond-shaped mass of gray matter in the anterior extremity of the temporal lobe; part of the limbic system
    https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/amygdala
  - Cambridge: one of two parts of the brain that affect how people feel emotions, especially fear and pleasure
    https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/amygdala
  - Oxford Learner’s: either of two areas in the brain that are linked to memory, the emotions and the sense of smell
    https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/amygdala

### basal ganglia
- 分类：神经科学
- 词性：plural noun / 复数名词
- English explanation: The basal ganglia are deep groups of gray-matter nuclei in each cerebral hemisphere that help regulate movement and also contribute to emotion, motivation, and cognition.
- 中文解释：基底神经节/基底核：位于大脑半球深部的一组灰质核团；对运动控制很重要，也参与情绪、动机和认知功能。
- 词典/公开来源：
  - Merriam-Webster: basal ganglion: any of four deeply placed masses of gray matter within each cerebral hemisphere
    https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/basal%20ganglion
  - Cambridge: basal ganglion: one of four areas in each side of the brain that helps process information related to movement, emotions, and solving problems
    https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/basal-ganglion?q=basal-ganglia

### brainstem
- 分类：神经科学
- 词性：noun / 名词
- English explanation: The brainstem is the central/lower part of the brain that connects the forebrain/cerebrum with the spinal cord; it includes the midbrain, pons, and medulla oblongata.
- 中文解释：脑干：连接大脑与脊髓的中下部脑结构，通常包括中脑、脑桥和延髓；参与呼吸、心率、觉醒等基本生命功能。
- 词典/公开来源：
  - Merriam-Webster: the part of the brain composed of the midbrain, pons, and medulla oblongata and connecting the spinal cord with the forebrain and cerebrum
    https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/brain%20stem
  - Cambridge: the part of the brain that connects the spinal cord to the forebrain
    https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/brainstem
  - Oxford Learner’s: the central part of the brain, which continues downwards to form the spinal cord
    https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/brainstem

### cerebellum
- 分类：神经科学
- 词性：noun / 名词
- English explanation: The cerebellum is the large structure at the back of the brain, behind/near the brainstem, involved especially in muscle coordination, movement, and balance.
- 中文解释：小脑：位于脑后部、脑干附近的大结构；主要参与运动协调、肌肉控制和平衡，也参与某些认知和学习过程。
- 词典/公开来源：
  - Merriam-Webster: a large dorsally projecting part of the brain concerned especially with the coordination of muscles and the maintenance of bodily equilibrium
    https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/cerebellum
  - Cambridge: a large part at the back of the brain that controls your muscles, movement, and balance
    https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/cerebellum
  - Oxford Learner’s: the part of the brain at the back of the head that controls the activity of the muscles
    https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/cerebellum

### hypothalamus
- 分类：神经科学
- 词性：noun / 名词
- English explanation: The hypothalamus is a small lower central part of the brain beneath the thalamus, containing autonomic and endocrine regulatory centers for body temperature, hunger, thirst, and hormone release.
- 中文解释：下丘脑：位于丘脑下方的脑区；调节体温、饥饿、口渴、内分泌激素释放以及自主神经功能。
- 词典/公开来源：
  - Merriam-Webster: a basal part of the diencephalon that lies beneath the thalamus... includes vital autonomic regulatory centers
    https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/hypothalamus
  - Cambridge: a small part in the brain that controls things such as body temperature and the release of hormones, that is below the thalamus
    https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/hypothalamus
  - Oxford Learner’s: an area in the central lower part of the brain that controls body temperature, hunger and the release of hormones
    https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/hypothalamus

### thalamus
- 分类：神经科学
- 词性：noun / 名词
- English explanation: The thalamus is a paired diencephalic structure that relays and processes information, especially sensory signals, to and from the cerebral cortex.
- 中文解释：丘脑：间脑中的成对结构，是许多感觉信息通往大脑皮层的重要中继站，也参与运动、觉醒和注意等功能。
- 词典/公开来源：
  - Merriam-Webster: the largest subdivision of the diencephalon... serves chiefly to relay impulses and especially sensory impulses to and from the cerebral cortex
    https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/thalamus
  - Cambridge: one of the two oval-shaped parts of the brain that control feeling and all the senses except for the sense of smell
    https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/thalamus
  - Oxford Learner’s: either of the two parts of the brain that control pain and feeling
    https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/thalamus
